2025 - 2026 Registration Calendar

Full Academic Year Registration

In April, students can register for a single semester or choose Full Academic Year Registration to enroll in multiple semesters at once for the upcoming Fall, Spring, and Summer terms.

2025-2026 Registration Opens

Currently enrolled students get early access to registration based on their student status or the number of credit hours they have completed. Registration opens for multiple semesters on the following dates:

AlamoPROMISE, Veterans/Active Military, Parenting Students, or have earned 46+ hours - March 28, 2025

31+ hours - April 2, 2025

9+ hours - April 4, 2025

All Students - April 7, 2025
