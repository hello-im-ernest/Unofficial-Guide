How are the academics at St. Philip's College?

Niche's Q&A highlights insights from students at St. Philip's College, answering common questions about academics, workload, professors, and class experience.

What academic support services and advising does St. Philip's College provide?

Students praise St. Philip's College for its strong commitment to student support services, including accessible and communicative advisors who help keep students on track with their degree plans. The college provides tutoring centers like 'Math World' and the 'Learning and Writing Center' that offer free assistance, which many students find invaluable for challenging subjects. Online students note that while some resources are limited compared to on-campus offerings, there is still support available via email, Zoom, and office hours.

How do students describe the teaching style and classroom environment at St. Philip's College?

St. Philip's College offers a welcoming and supportive academic environment where professors are known for being friendly, approachable, and engaging. Many students appreciate the small class sizes which allow for personalized attention and one-on-one interactions, making it easier to grasp course material and receive help. Professors often go above and beyond to assist students, making lectures interesting and fostering a positive learning experience.

What makes St. Philip's College's cultural and institutional identity unique?

St. Philip's College has a unique cultural identity as the only federally designated college to be both a Historically Black College and a Hispanic-Serving Institution. This dual designation fosters a diverse and inclusive atmosphere that celebrates multiple cultures and supports a wide range of students. The college emphasizes workforce development and social mobility, making it a vital educational and cultural resource within the San Antonio community.

How does St. Philip's College accommodate students with busy or non-traditional schedules?

The college provides flexibility in course formats, offering in-person, online, hybrid, and weekend classes to accommodate students with various schedules, especially those balancing work and family commitments. Online courses are generally well-structured and accessible, with professors who are attentive and responsive to students' questions. However, some students mention that consistency among online instructors could improve, particularly regarding exam schedules and communication.

What are some notable academic facilities and learning environments at St. Philip's College?

St. Philip's College features modern and specialized facilities, such as a new state-of-the-art Cybersecurity building that impresses students and enhances learning opportunities in technical fields. The college also offers hands-on learning environments, particularly in programs like Diesel Technology and Culinary Arts, where students engage in practical experience alongside classroom instruction. The campus is described as clean, well-maintained, and not too large, which students find comfortable and easy to navigate.
